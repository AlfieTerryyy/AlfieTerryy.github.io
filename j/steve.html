Steve's Ham-tastic Adventure

About Us: At Steve's, we believe in combining the unexpected. That's why we've merged two seemingly unrelated worlds: ham and kidnapping. Our founder, Steve, had a vision: to create a place where you can enjoy delicious cured meats while also experiencing the thrill of a lifetime. And thus, Steve's Local Kidnapper's Ham Shop & Van was born!

Our Menu:

  • Hammy Heists: Choose from our selection of premium hams, each with its own daring backstory. Will you go for the "Midnight Marauder" or the "Bacon Bandit"?
  • Van Specials: Our food truck, aptly named "The Getaway Van," serves up ham sandwiches with a side of adrenaline. Try the "Pulled Pork Pursuit" or the "Salami Stakeout."
  • Kidnapper's Combo: For the true thrill-seekers, we offer a combo meal that includes a ham sandwich, a getaway car keychain, and a simulated kidnapping experience. (Disclaimer: No actual kidnappings involved.)
